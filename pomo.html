About FlowFocus

FlowFocus is a Pomodoro timer designed to help you maintain focus and productivity. It uses the Pomodoro Technique, breaking work into intervals (typically 25 minutes) separated by short breaks.

Your data is stored locally using cookies and can be backed up to a file for safekeeping. The backup file contains your focus history for up to one year.
